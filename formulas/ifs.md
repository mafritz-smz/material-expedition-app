# IFS()

## Purpose

Implements conditional business rules.

## Usage

`IFS()` is used to determine whether a scanned piece satisfies the expedition requirements.

Typical validations include:

- Machine
- Lot
- Material
- Color

Depending on the result, the application either accepts or rejects the scan.

## Why It Is Used

Centralizing validation logic in expressions makes the application's behavior predictable and easier to maintain.
